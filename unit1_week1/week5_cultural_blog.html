Problems facing the Tech World: Burnout

Today with technology moving at a rapid pace, IT employees are constantly being challenged to incorporate the new advances in their software as well. Due to the fierce competition in the technological world there is a constant need to churn out quality & new software features to attract more customers. Every time a new technology comes into existence, IT employees are expected to learn it, master it and then produce software!

With complex software being developed there is also lot of possibilites of 'software bugs'. These bugs could lead to revenue loss and disconcerted customers. As a IT professional not only is creating new advanced software important but it has to be bug-free. This means thoroughly testing the software , fixing bugs and having multiple software releases.

Being in the IT industry currently I have experienced this first-hand. I personally feel IT Employees get overworked and underpaid. I hope things change in future!.
