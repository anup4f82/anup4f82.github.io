Blog: Thinking Style

My thinking style is Concrete sequential. After doing some research I found the below qualities for Concrete sequential thinkers:

  • Persistent and product oriented
  • "by the numbers" person
  • list-maker
  • Practical and Detail oriented
  • Instinctive,methodical,deliberate and structured
  • Environmental preference: ordered,practical,quiet and stable
  • Approach to life: Realistic,patient,conservative

After reading the qualities , I do relate to some of them. Anthony Gregorc has mentioned that no one has a 'pure' style which I agree to since I don't have some of the qualities listed above. Anthony Gregorc has mentioned a concrete sequential thinker likes Order,logical sequence and following directions. They way DBC has layed out the Phase 0 course structure with specific directions and sequence definitley helped me. I like getting directions and then working towards completing it. It helps me plan things accordingly.

Concrete sequential thinkers find it hard to:

  • Work in groups
  • Discussion with no specific point
  • Work in unorganized environment
  • Following incomplete or unclear directions
  • Working with unpredictable people
  • Dealing with abstract ideas
  • Demands to use your imagination

I can relate to some of the above qualities. I am a kind of person who likes to work on his own. I feel I can think and come up with solutions faster when I do it by myself. For some of the pairing sessions I felt I wasn't quick enough to understand concepts or come up with a solution. After the pairing session, when I tried doing it by myself, it clicked immdiately. This is one aspect that I want to improve on and get better at working in groups so I can excel at DBC.

Since I like Order and structure and following directions, I am sure it will help me at DBC to learn. I also like the fact that DBC is more practical oriented and I know I learn quicker that way. I also am sure I will be able to adapt to working in groups and figuring how to solve problems together.
