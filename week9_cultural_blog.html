Blog: Thinking Style

My thinking style is Concrete sequential. After doing some research I found the below qualities for Concrete sequential thinkers:

  • Persistent and product oriented
  • "by the numbers" person
  • list-maker
  • Practical and Detail oriented
  • Instinctive,methodical,deliberate and structured
  • Environmental preference: ordered,practical,quiet and stable
  • Approach to life: Realistic,patient,conservative

After reading the qualities , I do relate to some of them. Anthony Gregorc has mentioned that no one has a 'pure' style which I agree to since I don't have some of the qualities listed above. Anthony Gregorc has mentioned a concrete sequential thinker likes Order,logical sequence and following directions. The way DBC has laid out the Phase 0 course structure with specific directions and sequence definitely helped me. I like getting directions and then working towards completing it. It helps me plan things accordingly. I also work better when I am put under pressure. I like the fact that DBC holds us accountable if we are not upto par. This drives me to work harder so I can learn and finish my work on time.

Concrete sequential thinkers find it hard to:

  • Work in groups
  • Discussion with no specific point
  • Work in unorganized environment
  • Following incomplete or unclear directions
  • Working with unpredictable people
  • Dealing with abstract ideas
  • Demands to use your imagination

I can relate to some of the above qualities. Often at times I like to work on my own. I feel I can think and come up with solutions faster when I do it by myself. The pair programming sessions though have really helped me to break this barrier. All my pairs have been really patient and supportive. I am sure as I have more pair programming sessions at DBC I will work better in groups and excel at DBC

I am positive that I will be able to adapt at DBC and learn and improve my technical skills. This is a new phase in my life and a huge commitment on my part. I want to make this experience as productive as I can and I am sure with help of my peers and DBC I will achieve my goals.
